Welcome to Woodland Rise Camping and Caravan park!

We're a small, family-run park in the rural hamlet of Blaen-y-coed in Carmarthenshire, West Wales. In our quiet countryside location (approximately 6 miles from Carmarthen), visitors can enjoy views of the gorgeous West Wales countryside by day and starry skies by night. We are a pet-friendly site, with plenty of space onsite and a local woodland trail for you and your furry friends to stretch your legs.
